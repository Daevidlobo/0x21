From this world of Decentralized Finance have been born numerous opportunities for yield and seed-stage investment that have been yet untapped by the vast majority. 0x21 is an investment firm committed to exceptional returns for investors through actively managed portfolios of these blockchain assets, built by companies and protocols fueling the decentralization of the web, society, and finance.
