Investment and Acceleration

Traditional funds are limited by the markets and frameworks in which they operate and will typically focus on either venture investment or asset management.

The flexibility of the DeFi sector enables 0x21 to take a dual approach, capitalizing on existing opportunities, and incubating for the future.

We put investors funds to work immediately across a range of existing protocols, whilst incubating and supporting the next wave of market movers.

Investment

The flexibility of DeFi enables us to immediately deploy investor funds into highly lucrative opportunities across stable coins, delta neutral strategies, and higher risk yield farming opportunities. Our team of experts constantly monitors and manages positions to optimize returns.

Accelerator

We identify the hottest trends in DeFi before the masses. We leverage our extensive network to find the brightest minds to build solutions. We support them with the funding, infrastructure and relationships that position 0x21 incubated protocols ahead of the competition.
